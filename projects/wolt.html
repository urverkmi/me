Your city, Wrapped

Hackathon Front-end Dev Figma Make Group project

Developed for Wolt's challenge during Junction 2025, a 48h hackathon. My main role was front-end development, integrating the back-end algorithm with the Figma prototype.

City exploration

Wolt's challenge was to create a feature that would encourage more local experiences. To address this, we envisioned a tab where the app helps you understand your habits and discover new favourites.

We implemented a machine learning algorithm that generates personalized quests based on a weighted rating of the dishes based on the user's prior ordering history.

Wolt wrapped

After completing the quests, the user has the option to create a "Wrapped" summary of their culinary adventures and share to social media.
